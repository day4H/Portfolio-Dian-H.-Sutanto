About Me

I'm Dian Handayani Sutanto, a graduate of SMK Negeri 1 Cileungsi majoring in Visual Communication Design. I’m passionate about graphic design, photography, and digital content creation. Over the past few years, I’ve been involved in various creative projects including logo design, merchandise mockups, social media content, and visual documentation for both organizations and industries. I’m someone who thrives in collaborative environments, adapts quickly, and pays strong attention to visual detail. To me, design is not just about aesthetics, but also about delivering messages in a clear and meaningful way. I’m eager to keep learning and growing so I can contribute effectively in a professional work setting.
